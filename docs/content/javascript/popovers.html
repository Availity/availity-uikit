--- title: Popovers description: | Popovers are small modular windows, which appear on top of other content. While tooltips provide a small amount of information on hover, popovers provide space for greater amounts of information and should be triggered on click. Because they're triggered by click, and don't rely on mouse position (as they would on hover), popovers may contain links and other small interactive content. usage: | Use popovers wherever you want to provide contextual information that goes beyond the scope of a tooltip, or which needs to appear even when a user is not hovered over the content. Popovers can be shown without a user's direct action, such as to highlight a new feature, but should be used sparingly in this manner. ---
