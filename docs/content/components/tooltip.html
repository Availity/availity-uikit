--- title: Tooltips description: | Tooltips provide text-only contextual information on hover. They are particularly useful for providing small tidbits of information, such as a definition of a term or an additional explanation of what an action does. usage: | Use tooltips wherever you need to provide additional contextual information to reinforce or enhance primary content. An example usage could be on hover of a disabled button, a tooltip can be used to explain why the button is disabled. ---
