--- title: Navbar description: | The primary nav bar sits below the utility bar and contains primary navigation for the system, including both Availity workflow apps and Spaces, as well as user Favorites. usage: | The primary nav bar should always be shown, and should not vary between pages. notes: | In very rare cases, such as a specialized wizard workflow, it may be acceptable to modify or hide the primary nav bar. This behavior, if relevant, should be codified in a separate pattern. ---
